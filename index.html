Recommended tier logic

Standard/Pro → Foundation. Pro Plus → Advanced. Enterprise+ → Advanced, unless Heavy intensity OR 5+ products (then → Prime). Heavy + 5+ products bumps one level. Prime caps the recommendation.

Risk score (0–100)

Compares projected Now Assist consumption against the included pool at the recommended tier. Intensity × seats × 12 months ÷ pool capacity. Below 35 = low, 35–70 = medium, above 70 = high.

What this tool doesn't model

Multi-year ramps, vertical SKUs (FSO, HCLS-SM, MCO, TSM), Security SKUs (USEM, SIR), IRM/BCM/PRM add-ons, IntegrationHub spokes, OT/industrial site pricing, WDF Data Fabric packs, non-prod sandbox multipliers.
